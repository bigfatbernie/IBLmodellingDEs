\emph{Question.} 
What is the path of a lion chasing an antelope?

\vfill

\begin{graybox}
\emph{Rules. } 
\begin{itemize}
\item The antelope flees at a constant speed $v$ in a straight line
\item The lion chases at a constant speed $u$
\end{itemize}
\end{graybox}
